U of I prepared me to do that with classwork that taught us how to use data analysis tools that can be used on problems that don’t have answers in the back of the book. My course selections were chosen to give me a strong background in quantitative analytics, working primarily with R, Excel, and Python to compile the data needed to better understand questions and find the answers to those questions.
